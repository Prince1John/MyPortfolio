Awareness created by my Projects

Posters and flyers play a crucial role in marketing, communication, and customer engagement at Ektron Insurance Company. They are used both internally (for employees) and externally (for clients and prospects). Below is a breakdown of their key applications:

  • Internal Communication: Posters and flyers are used to inform employees about company updates, events, and training sessions.
  • External Marketing: They serve as tools to promote new insurance products, discounts, and campaigns to clients and prospects.
  • Brand Awareness: Professionally designed materials help reinforce the company's brand identity and values.
  • Event Promotion: Flyers are distributed to advertise seminars, workshops, and community outreach programs organized by the company.
